How to Play

1. Start the Game: Click on any cell to begin. The cell will reveal its contents.

2. Uncover Cells: Click on cells to uncover them. Numbers indicate how many mines are adjacent to that cell.

3. Flag Mines: Right-click to place a flag on cells you suspect contain mines. This helps avoid accidental clicks.

4. Avoid Mines: Try to uncover all cells without clicking on any mines. If you uncover a mine, the game is over.

5. Win the Game: Successfully uncover all non-mine cells to win. Use logic and deduction to avoid mines and reveal safe areas.
